A precise, no-nonsense tool for generating and validating .cue files from disc images. Built for archivists, preservationists, and anyone who needs their cues done right.

Disc images deserve proper cues

Every disc image needs a companion .cue file to describe its track layout, sector sizes, and session boundaries. Without one, your archive is incomplete. cue-me ensures every .bin, .iso, and .img gets a precisely formatted cue sheet — automatically, correctly, every time.

No guesswork. No hand-editing. Just clean, spec-compliant cue files generated from your disc images with a single command.

What it does

Six focused capabilities. No bloat. No dependencies you don't need.

01

Auto-Generate

Scan directories recursively and generate .cue files for every compatible disc image in one pass.

02

Smart Detection

Automatically detects format, track layout, and sector size. No manual configuration for standard images.

03

Validation

Verify existing cue files against their disc images. Catch mismatches before they corrupt your archive.

04

Metadata

Extract and display track counts, formats, sizes, and session info with a single command.

05

Format Coverage

Works with BIN/CUE, ISO, IMG, and other common disc formats. Handles multiple sector sizes.

06

Speed

Process hundreds of files in seconds. Minimal memory footprint. Built for large collections.
